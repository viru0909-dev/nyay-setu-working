Section 9 in The Right to Information Act, 2005 9. Grounds for rejection to access in certain cases. - Without prejudice to the provisions of section 8, a Central Public Information Officer or a State Public Information Officer, as the case may be, may reject a request for information where such a request for providing access would involve an infringement of copyright subsisting in a person other than the State.
