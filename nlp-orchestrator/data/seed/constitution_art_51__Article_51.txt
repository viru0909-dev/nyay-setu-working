Article 51 in Constitution of India 51. Promotion of international peace and security The State shall endeavour to--(a)promote international peace and security;(b)maintain just and honourable relations between nations;(c)foster respect for international law and treaty obligations in the dealings of organised peoples with one another; and(d)encourage settlement of international disputes by arbitration.
