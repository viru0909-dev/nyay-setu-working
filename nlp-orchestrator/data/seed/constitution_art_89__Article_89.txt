Article 89 in Constitution of India 89. The Chairman and Deputy Chairman of the Council of States (1)The Vice-President of India shall be ex-officio Chairman of the Council of States.(2)The Council of States shall, as soon as may be, choose a member of the Council to be Deputy Chairman thereof and, so often as the office of Deputy Chairman becomes vacant, the Council shall choose anothermember to be Deputy Chairman thereof.
